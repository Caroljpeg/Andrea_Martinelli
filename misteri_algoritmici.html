/ extra
feelings dictionary
misteri algoritmici is an intermedial translation born out of the desire to give a new form to the collection of short stories la boutique del mistero by dino buzzati. the idea originates in a particular characteristic of buzzati's narrative style, which is capable of suggesting rather than telling, depicting stories that are made up not of events but of sensations that pass and disappear or settle, determining a leitmotif.
in a preliminary phase, these sensations were defined and each of the thirty-one stories that constitute misteri algoritmici was re-written as a list of sensations. each sensation was then encoded as a sound, generating a new sonic alphabet that could be precisely controlled in each of its parameters through the use of a code-based music production library (sonic pi).
