/ andrea martinelli

/ profile

digital designer and audiovisual artist with a strong passion for the expressive possibilities offered by computational methodologies and interdisciplinary practices. Graduated with a bachelor's degree in visual design from Politecnico di Milano in 2023, after working in Bruxelles and Milan he's currently pursuing a master's degree in the Geo-Design department of the Design Academy Eindhoven.
