original version (fr)
poisson fuyant is an animated short movie, realised with a mixed media technique. it combines traditional, 3D animation and stop motion, turning its formal incoherence into the starting point for a narrative that is as fundamental as universal.
it is an ode to the eternal discomfort of those who feel surrounded by a reality whose meaning they do not recognise, even when someone tries to explain it to them.
