teaser
mutu cu sapi lu jocu (literally "those who know the answer to the riddle, keep silent") talks about lives, about faith, dissatisfaction, roots, attachment.
salvatore, the protagonist, is left unsatisfied, disenchanted and tormented in a reality in which he experiences discomfort.
he feels stung by everything around him, stuck in the remembrance of a past and a first love lost forever.
he is not able to find a way out. Or perhaps he does not want to. Or perhaps he cannot.
